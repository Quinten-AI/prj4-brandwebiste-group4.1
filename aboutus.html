About us

about SPARK

Spark was created from the feeling of being stuck. Being in a cycle and unable to find new experiences. We decided to turn this around, and created…SPARK! The concept is simple: every month, you get a message with 4 options to choose from, that month’s new experiences. These can differ from a new recipe, to a coupon for a new sport. You pick one, and only pay for that one! Don’t feel like a new thing one month? No problem! If you don’t pick one, we’ll just be back the next month with new options. Make sure to check out our product page for more info!

OUR VALUES

Spark lives by CURIOSITY
We believe in the freedom to try new experiences whenever possible, as curiosity is the fuel for human advancement.

Spark lives by EXPLORATION
We value the Exploration that is sparked by curiosity, ashistory has shown us that innovation and human expansion are driven by exploration.

Spark lives by the EXTRA-ORDINARY
We aspire to spark the Extra-Ordinary in your life, as living an ordinary life closes doors that will only open if you dare to live the EXTRA life.

Spark lives by OPEN-MINDEDNESS
We strive for the acceptance of mindsets and cultures from around the world, as we believe that every culture adds to the beauty of the world as a whole.
