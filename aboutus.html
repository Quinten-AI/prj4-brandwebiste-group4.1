About us

Why do we SPARK?

Spark was created from the feeling of being stuck. Being in a cycle and unable to find new experiences. We decided to turn this around, and created…SPARK! The concept is simple: every month, you get a message with 4 options to choose from, that month’s new experiences. These can differ from a new recipe, to a coupon for a new sport. You pick one, and only pay for that one! Don’t feel like a new thing one month? No problem! If you don’t pick one, we’ll just be back the next month with new options. Make sure to check out our product page for more info!

Sharing Our Mission

  • At Spark, we strive to make people experience new things. Step out of their comfort zone and with this, create the extraordinary life they desire. At Spark, we strive to make people experience new things. Step out of their comfort zone and with this, create the extraordinary life they desire.

  • We do this by providing our customers with a new experience every month. Through this, the only thing that is left to do is: experience and discover that extraordinary life!

Sharing our vision

When you feel outcasted as most people accept the ordinary life, we believe that you can spark the unexpected in your life only when you explore the undiscovered, because then can you create the extraordinary life you desire.
