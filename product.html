Are you ready to be sparked?

At SPARK we want to offer you a range of products, so to keep you curious. Therefore we offer a subscription service. Every month you get a box based on your interests. Each box will contain different products that give you an opportunity to experience something new. The options vary from a cocktail workshop, exotic recipes, a voucher for skydiving, and you also get discount codes to explore other activities/products! If you want to give your box to someone else as a present, there is also an option to do that. Give someone else a present to spark their curiosity. It's different products in every box, every month. Be ready to be SPARKED, to be curious, to explore.
